# E201 Vibration Maintenance Manual

E201 indicates abnormal vibration on rotating equipment such as motors, pumps, and compressors.

Common causes include loose mounting bolts, bearing wear, shaft misalignment, rotor imbalance, abnormal load, or mechanical resonance.

Inspection steps:

1. Check whether vibration exceeds the normal operating threshold.
2. Inspect mounting bolts, base plate, coupling, and bearing condition.
3. Compare vibration trend with current, temperature, and load changes.
4. Reduce load or stop the device if vibration continues to rise.
5. Arrange on-site maintenance before restarting high-load operation.
